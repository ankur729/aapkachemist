Medicine Questions

Q.1 Are medications available at Aapka Chemist safe?

Yes. All medications provided by Aapka Chemist are sourced from licenced and certified pharmacies.

Q.2 What is the meaning of Generic Drugs?

Generic Drugs, are drugs that which can be marketed without brand names. These durgs are considered identical as their brand-name counterparts in all respects such as strength, dose, active ingredients, intended use, route of administration, efficacy, bioavailability and safety. As required by the CDSCO, India’s regulatory body for pharmaceuticals, Generics must meet the same rigid standards as set for brand-name counterparts.
